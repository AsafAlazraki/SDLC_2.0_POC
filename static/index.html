💡 HOW THE DISCOVERY ENGINE WORKS

A peak under the hood of our Multi-Agent Hybrid Intelligence platform.

📡
1. Repository Ingestion

Our engine clones the repository and performs a deep recursive scan, filtering for source code while skipping binary artifacts and dependencies. Content is aggregated into a high-density intelligence context.

🤖
2. Fleet Dispatch

We launch a fleet of 11+ specialized AI personas. Each agent is a distinct instance of either **Claude Sonnet 4.6** (for complex logic/strategy) or **Gemini 2.0 Flash** (for deep research and grounding).

🔍
3. Grounded Research

Gemini agents use Google Search grounding to research the tech stack, library vulnerabilities, and industry standards, ensuring the analysis isn't just a hallucination but a verified audit.

🏗️
4. Synthesis & Visualization

The Architect agent synthesizes the findings into a phased roadmap, while the BA generates a structured Jira backlog. Mermaid.js is utilized to render real-time architectural diagrams.
